Programs & Services Overview

Counselling and Support Services of S.D. & G. offers bilingual counselling and support services to individuals, couples, parents and families for the purpose of alleviating problem situations and encouraging positive personal growth. The agency experienced counsellors and case managers are committed to promoting individual, partner, parental and family well being. This support leads to healthy relationships and strong communities.

United Way funding enables the agengy to deliver quality counselling to our clients at an affordable cost. A reasonable fee for service is discussed and established with the Social Worker during your first initial interview.

The agengy's Case Management services are funded by the Ministry of Community and Social Services; there is no fee for service. Call and the agency intake workers will review and assess your needs over the phone and let you know when the next available appointment is. There is, at times, a waiting period to come in and see a worker face to face.
